Wiring and behavior

Profiles select which pump they use. Only one pump moves at a time. Pump 2 uses STEP/DIR/ENABLE 5/13/14; Pump 3 uses 22/15/2. See Hardware for the complete map.

Safety

When asserted, all pumps stop, valves close, and the system enters a fault.

UART wiring details

All drivers share RX/TX GPIO 16/17 and use addresses 0b00, 0b01, and 0b10.

Danger zone

Factory reset

Erases all pump profiles, calibrations, and hardware settings, then reboots. This cannot be undone. Normal firmware and web-interface updates do not erase this data.
