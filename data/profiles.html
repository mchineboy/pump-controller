Fluid profiles

Six generic slots (Fluid 1–6). Rename them for hydroponics, laundry, chemistry, or any other fluid path. Each keeps its own calibration, speed, and limits.

Valve (optional)

Valve timing applies only when valve hardware is enabled in Diagnostics settings and a matched valve driver is wired. Do not drive a piezo valve from GPIO directly.

Changing anti-drip settings clears calibration; recalibrate before dispensing.
